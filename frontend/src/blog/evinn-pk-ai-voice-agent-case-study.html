How we built an AI voice agent for Evinn.pk to handle 80+ daily calls

AI Voice Agents Jun 19, 2025 · 6 min read
Quick Answer
By deploying a custom vector-indexed voice agent connected directly to Evinn.pk's GoHighLevel CRM, we automated 24/7 call answering, dropped manual lead management times by 70%, and captured inbound leads that previously went to voicemail.

For service-based companies and retailers, every phone call is a potential high-value sale. Yet, maintaining a dedicated, round-the-clock receptionist team is cost-prohibitive. Evinn.pk, an electric scooter vendor, faced this exact challenge: their 3-person staff was overwhelmed by 80+ customer queries per day, resulting in missed leads and slow sales velocity.

The Opportunity: Zero Response Delay

When a prospective client calls to check inventory sizes or warranty terms, they expect immediate answers. A delay of just five minutes in returning a voicemail drops connection likelihood by over 80%. We built a customized AI voice agent designed to handle inbound queries, qualify customers, and push hot prospects directly to live sales reps.

"Our primary objective was to eliminate the voicemail bottleneck entirely. We needed a system that answered in under 600ms, maintained a consistent brand tone, and synced customer data directly."

Building the System Core

The voice agent is built on a custom vector database mapping all product specifications. We configured a low-latency telephony server connected to a Twilio trunk, with webhooks pushing lead cards to the CRM. The core logic handles dynamic schema checks in real-time.

Here is a sample data block representing the call outcome sync webhook:

{ "call_id": "c_2025_ef99a2", "qualified": true, "lead_score": 92, "action": "book_appointment" }

The Results: 70% Time Savings

Within two weeks of switching the primary business lines to the AI agent:

1. **0 Missed Calls:** 100% of incoming customer inquiries were picked up on the first ring, 24/7.
2. **70% Handle Time Drop:** Routine FAQ calls were answered automatically, freeing human reps to focus strictly on closing qualified deals.
3. **3x Demos Booked:** Seamless CRM scheduling integrations allowed callers to reserve test rides without manual intervention.

Key Learnings

Successful voice AI implementation requires strict script boundaries. By limiting the model's knowledge base to vector-indexed documents, we prevented conversational hallucination and maintained strict brand guidelines across thousands of calls.
